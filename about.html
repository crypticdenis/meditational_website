About Meditational

What is Meditational?

Meditational is a free, simple online meditation timer designed for focus, calm, and clarity. It provides an elegant interface with customizable session durations, optional interval bells, and ambient background sounds like river and forest environments.

Why This Tool Exists

Most meditation apps are bloated with features, require accounts, and push subscriptions or ads. Meditational is built for people who want to meditate without distractions. No logins, no tracking, just a clean space for breathing, stillness, and silence.

About Meditational

What is Meditational?

Meditational is a free, simple online meditation timer designed for focus, calm, and clarity.
It provides an elegant interface with customizable session durations, optional interval bells,
and ambient background sounds like river and forest environments.

Why This Tool Exists

Most meditation apps are bloated with features, require accounts, and push subscriptions or ads.
Meditational is built for people who want to meditate without distractions. No logins, no tracking,
just a clean space for breathing, stillness, and silence.

Key Features

  • Simple meditation timer with start/pause/reset
  • Optional interval bells
  • Background nature sounds
  • Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers
  • 100% free, with no ads or tracking

Who It's For

Meditational is ideal for:

  • People practicing mindfulness or Zen meditation
  • Yoga teachers or students needing timed sessions
  • Anyone seeking a quiet space for reflection or focus
  • Digital minimalists who want function without noise

How to Use

Set your desired meditation time, choose an interval bell if needed, optionally enable nature sounds,
and press start. That’s it. You can meditate in silence or with sound, eyes open or closed.

Privacy-First Design

We do not collect any personal data, use cookies, or show ads. What happens in your meditation session stays there.

Simple Online Meditation Timer

Meditational is a free, minimalist meditation timer designed for calm and focus. Whether you're practicing mindfulness, doing yoga, or simply trying to relax, this tool gives you a distraction-free space with interval bells and nature background sounds like river or forest.

Use it as a guided timer for your meditation sessions, with customizable durations and optional interval alerts. There's nothing to install, no sign-up, and no ads—just a clean, calming interface to support your practice.

Features

  • Customizable timer settings
  • Interval bells for mindfulness practice
  • Calming nature sounds
  • No sign-up required
  • Completely free to use
